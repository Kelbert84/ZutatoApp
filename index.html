Zutato – Your smart recipe scanner app

❗️ The Android version is currently in active development.

With Zutato, you can easily scan, digitize, and manage handwritten or photographed recipes. No login, no tracking — everything stays on your device or securely in your iCloud.

📲 View on the App Store

📲 Features

  • Scan recipes via camera (OCR)
  • Edit or enter recipes manually
  • Rate recipes with 1–5 stars
  • Categorize & filter by rating or category
  • Share recipes as PDF (incl. image, ingredients, instructions)
  • Add photo, export PDF, iCloud storage

📄 Privacy Policy

Zutato stores your recipes and images only on your device or your private iCloud (via Apple CloudKit).

Collected Data

  • Recipes & Images: Stored locally or in iCloud only
  • No login: No account required
  • No tracking: No ads, no analytics

Your Rights

  • You can delete all data at any time — in the app or via iCloud settings
